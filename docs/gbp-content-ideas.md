# Nick's Tire & Auto — Google Business Profile Content Ideas

**Goal:** Stay active on GBP to improve local pack rankings, drive direct calls, and show
Google that this is an active, engaged business. Post 2–4 times per week.

---

## CONTENT PILLARS

1. **Trust & Transparency** — Show the work. Photos before/after, parts shown to customers.
2. **Local Cleveland Relevance** — Weather, seasons, local events, Cleveland driver problems.
3. **Educational** — "Why does this happen?" in plain English. No jargon.
4. **Social Proof** — Real reviews, real outcomes, real savings.
5. **Offers & Urgency** — Seasonal deals, limited specials, time-sensitive reminders.

## PHOTO CONTENT GUIDE

| Type | Frequency | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bay photos (work in progress) | 2–3x/week | Show the actual work — not stock photos |
| Parts comparison (before/after) | 1–2x/week | Worn vs. new is the most shareable format |
| Customer pickup moment | 1x/week | Get permission. Capture the handoff. |
| Team photos | 2x/month | Real people build trust |
| Shop exterior (seasonal) | 1x/month | Shows you're open, local, active |
| Review screenshots | 1–2x/week | Resize with a clean background |
