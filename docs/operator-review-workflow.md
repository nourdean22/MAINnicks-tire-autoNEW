# Nick's Tire & Auto — Review & Reputation Operator Workflow

**Owner:** Front desk / manager
**Time required:** 15–20 minutes per day
**Tools needed:** Google Business Profile (browser or app)

---

## DAILY ROUTINE (15 minutes)

### Step 1 — Check for new reviews (5 min)
1. Open Google Business Profile
2. Go to Reviews tab
3. Sort by "Newest first"
4. Read every new review since yesterday

### Step 2 — Respond to reviews (10 min)
1. For **positive reviews:** Use template from `review-response-templates.md`
   - Pick the matching category (Gratitude, Honesty, Speed, etc.)
   - Personalize with at least ONE detail from their review
   - Keep it under 3 sentences unless the review was long and detailed
   - Target response time: within 48 hours

2. For **negative reviews:** RESPOND SAME DAY
   - Do not argue or explain in the public response
   - Acknowledge → apologize → invite offline conversation
   - Use Templates NC-1, NC-2, NC-3 from the template library
   - Alert the manager immediately for any review mentioning:
     - Safety concern
     - Specific employee by name (positive or negative)
     - Request for refund
     - Potential legal language

3. For **fake or unfair reviews:**
   - Use Template NU-1 or NU-2
   - Flag to manager for potential GBP dispute filing
   - Document: date, reviewer name, content

---

## WEEKLY ROUTINE (30 minutes)

### Monday morning — Post GBP content
1. Pick one post from `gbp-content-ideas.md`
2. Pair with a real photo from the shop (bay, parts, team)
3. Publish via Google Business Profile > Add Update

### Friday — Review audit
1. Count new reviews this week
2. Note what topics customers praised most
3. Share any standout reviews with the team (morale + reinforcement)

---

## ESCALATION RULES

| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| Review mentions unsafe work | Manager reviews immediately, document, call customer |
| Review from someone who isn't in our records | Respond per Template NU-1, flag for GBP dispute |
| Same customer left multiple negatives | Flag for manager, consider GBP abuse report |
| Review mentions a specific tech negatively | Private conversation with tech first, then respond publicly |
| 3+ new negatives in one week | Emergency meeting — investigate root cause |

## REVIEW GENERATION REMINDERS

### At vehicle pickup (best moment)
> "If you're happy with the service today, a quick Google review means a lot to us — it helps other Cleveland drivers find a shop they can trust."

### QR code at counter
- Print and laminate a QR code linking directly to the GBP review page
- Place at counter, on invoices, and near the exit

### Follow-up text (if customer opted in)
> "Hi [Name], this is Nick's Tire & Auto. Hope your [service] is running great. If you have a moment, a Google review helps other Cleveland drivers find us: [short link]. Thanks for your business."
