# Nick's Tire & Auto — Review Response Template Library

**Purpose:** Consistent, human, on-brand responses to Google Business Profile reviews.
**Voice:** Direct, warm, grateful. Never corporate. Never defensive. Always specific.
**Rule:** Personalize each response with at least one detail from the actual review.

## USAGE NOTES FOR OPERATORS

1. **Always use the customer's first name.** Never "Dear Customer."
2. **Reference at least one specific detail** from their review. Generic responses read as automated.
3. **Never argue.** Even for unfair reviews, stay factual and offer to connect offline.
4. **Response time target:** Within 24 hours for negative reviews, 48–72 hours for positive.
5. **Signature:** Always end with "— Nick's Team" or "— [Your name], Nick's Tire & Auto"
6. **No marketing language** in responses. No "We pride ourselves on…" or "Our commitment to excellence…"
7. **Flag to manager:** Any review mentioning a specific tech by name, any refund request, any safety concern.
