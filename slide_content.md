## Slide 4: Sprint 1A — Booking System UI Overhaul
**Title:** A Better Booking Experience That Keeps You in Control
**The problem:** Current booking form works but looks basic. First impressions matter — customers judge your shop by your website.
**The solution — Multi-step booking form:**
- Step 1: Vehicle info (year/make/model dropdowns)
- Step 2: Service selection (checkboxes with descriptions)
- Step 3: Describe the problem (text + optional photo upload)
- Step 4: Contact details + confirmation
**Key principle:** First-come, first-serve stays. No time slots. No calendar. Customer submits, you decide the order.
**Confirmation message:** "Request received. We service vehicles on a first-come, first-serve basis. We'll reach out when your vehicle is next in line."
**Admin upgrade:** Drag-to-reorder job queue. Flag high-profit jobs. Sort by service type, date, or vehicle.

## Slide 5: Sprint 1B — "What's Wrong With My Car?" Diagnostic Tool
**Title:** Get Customers In the Door Without Quoting a Price
**The strategy:** Customers want to know what's wrong. They don't need a price — they need confidence that you can fix it. This tool gives them that confidence and drives them to your shop.
**How it works:**
- Customer selects symptoms: shaking, grinding noise, warning light, smell, pulling to one side
- AI generates a plain-language explanation of likely causes
- Shows related service page (links to /brakes, /diagnostics, etc.)
- Ends with strong CTA: "Come in for a free diagnostic inspection — (216) 862-0005"
**Why no prices:** You close the customer in person. The tool builds trust and gets them through the door. The diagnosis sells the job.
**SEO bonus:** Each symptom combination creates a unique page that Google can index — targeting searches like "car shaking while driving Cleveland"

## Slide 9: Sprint 3A — Coupon & Specials System
**Title:** Vague But Clever — Specials That Drive Curiosity
**The philosophy:** Never give away the price. Create curiosity that gets them through the door.
**Examples:**
- "First-time customer? Ask about our new customer special."
- "Mention this ad for a seasonal brake inspection deal."
- "Military and first responder discounts available — ask us."
- "Refer a friend and both of you save."
**Admin controls:**
- Create/edit/delete promotions from the admin dashboard
- Set expiration dates and auto-rotation
- Assign promotions to specific service pages
- Track how many people mention the coupon code
**Display:** Subtle banner on relevant service pages. Not flashy — professional and confident.

## Slide 10: Sprint 3B & 3C — Shop Status Widget + Retention Tools
**Title:** Real-Time Transparency + Automated Customer Retention
**Shop Status Widget:**
- Simple admin toggle on the dashboard: "Open Bays Available" / "Moderate Wait" / "Busy — Call Ahead"
- Displays on homepage — creates urgency without giving away too much
- You update it when you want, or leave it off entirely
- Builds trust: "This shop is transparent about their workload"
**Automated Follow-Up System:**

- 3-month reminder for oil changes, 6-month for brakes, 12-month for major repairs
- Personalized: "Hi [Name], your last oil change was 5 months ago on your 2018 Honda Accord"
- Drives reviews + repeat visits on autopilot
